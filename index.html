Questions customers will ask.

Clear answers for customers before they book.

Do I need to provide water?

The service is designed around water-efficient cleaning. If a specific deep clean requires extra water, we confirm it before the booking.

Is the process safe for paint?

The cleaner uses microfiber cloths and car-safe products. We avoid dry wiping and check the car condition before the service starts.

Can you clean cars in an apartment or office parking area?

Yes, subject to building or office permission. Building plans are preferred because multiple cars can be serviced in one visit.

What happens if I am not satisfied?

You can inspect the car before payment. If something obvious is missed, we ask the cleaner to correct it before closing the job.
